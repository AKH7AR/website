THE NOT SO FAVOURITE DISHES



For anyone who knows me, knows that there is a lot i dont eat, like ALOT!. Sushi, ok my friend, the issue with sushi is that its still dancing on my plate when its time to eat, i just cant do it! Pasta, controversial this one but its gotta be done. Well to be frank there is nothing wrong with pasta at all, but for myself regardless of how you try and dress the dish up... after eating it you always look like you made love to a clown, no i cant be doing that. keep it simple and basic but always tasty is the motto! there are many dishes i dont like for the simple fact youve tried to make grandma look sixteen again, leave the poor girl alone and live her days out! this is how i feel for cheese as an examle, mozzerella, cream cheese etc all fine and then you need to turn it blue , green and the rest of it.. why?!!?
